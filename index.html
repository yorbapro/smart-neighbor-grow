What Is an AI Receptionist?

An AI receptionist is an artificial intelligence system that answers phone calls for your business automatically — just like a human receptionist, but available 24/7/365. It handles appointment booking, lead qualification, call routing, FAQ answering, and after-hours coverage at a fraction of the cost of hiring staff.

BrightLaunchIQ AI Receptionist Features

  • 24/7 AI call answering — never miss a call again
  • Appointment booking with real-time calendar integration
  • Lead qualification with custom intake questions
  • Intelligent call routing and live call transfer
  • After-hours, weekend, and holiday coverage
  • CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot, Zoho)
  • Call transcription, summaries, and recordings
  • SMS and email confirmations
  • Custom branded greeting
  • Multi-location support
  • HIPAA-ready configurations for healthcare

AI Receptionist vs Hiring Staff

A full-time receptionist costs $35,000–$55,000+ per year, works limited hours, and calls in sick. BrightLaunchIQ AI Receptionist works 24/7/365 at a predictable monthly rate — no payroll taxes, no benefits, no overtime, no turnover.

AI Receptionist vs Virtual Receptionist

A virtual receptionist is a remote human costing $250–$500+/month with per-call fees and limited hours. BrightLaunchIQ AI Receptionist answers instantly 24/7 with no per-call fees and handles unlimited simultaneous calls.

AI Receptionist vs Answering Service

Traditional answering services take messages. BrightLaunchIQ AI Receptionist books appointments, qualifies leads, routes urgent calls, and handles FAQs — all automatically, with no hold times and no per-minute fees.
